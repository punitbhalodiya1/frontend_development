NEW CATEGORIES & PRICING

The conversation based pricing model will cover four types of conversations instead of the current two - User initiated and Business Initiated Conversations starting June 01, 2023.

Service conversations

Formerly known as UIC (User initiated conversation)

All user-initiated conversations will now be categorized as service conversations, which help customers resolve enquiries. Encourage more engagement with high returns on investment

Implications:

No price change.

Lower support costs leading to higher ROI

The Business initiated conversations, which need to start with a template, will now be categorized in to -

Utility conversations

Templates that facilitate a specific, agreed-upon request or transaction or update to a customer about an ongoing transaction, including post-purchase notifications and recurring billing statements.

Implications:

Proven ROI for operational efficiencies

93% reduction in contact volume across other service channels

Stay in touch with your customers and give them updates at a much lower rate* (*varies by geographic location)

Check prices here

Authentication conversations

Templates that enable businesses to authenticate users with one-time passcodes, potentially at multiple steps in the login process (e.g., account verification, account recovery, integrity challenges)

Implications:

Opportunity to bring SMS-based OTP messages on WhatsApp

More secure because of encrypted messages.

Easy access

Driving high ROI for OTP and authentication use-cases

Although not revealed by META, conversation prices will be lower than utility messages.

Marketing conversations

Templates that include promotions or offers, informational updates, or invitations for customers to respond / take action.

Any conversation that does not qualify as utility or authentication is a marketing conversation.

Implications:

~2X ROI for Sales & Commerce

67% lift in Conversion rate

Enriched engagement features coming up making marketing interactive and delightful

Although prices are going to increase; this will avoid WhatsApp from becoming a spam channel & thus provide increased ROI on campaigns.

Check prices here

WHATS MORE?
1

Additionally, the free entry point window has been extended from 1 to 3 days when a conversation starts from Facebook or Instagram ads that Click to WhatsApp or a Facebook page CTA for all businesses. This has been effective since March 01, 2023, as informed in one of our recent newsletters.
2

Only Service conversations will be considered for the 1000 Free Tier Conversations allowed per month per WABA, and not the conversations initiated by the business such as Utility, Authentication or Marketing, applicable from June 01, 2023
3

For all four conversation types, the 24 hour session window will be considered as a separate window from June 01, 2023, unlike currently. E.g. If a Marketing conversation window is opened at 3.40pm of Day 1 and charged accordingly, followed by a Utility conversation opened at 5.15pm of Day 1, it will still open a separate Utility conversation window charged accordingly. However, if a second Marketing conversation is sent again at 11am on Day 2, it will not be charged again till 3.40pm Day 2.

PREPARING FOR THE CHANGES

Alert : Might require some technical changes before 28th March, 2023

Meta will start migrating all existing templates from April 01, 2023 into the above relevant categories, but to be prepared for this change in advance, Gupshup will be enabling the aspect starting March 29, 2023 -
Note : There will be no change in the Template ID

Creating templates

Starting March 29, 2023, users will need to submit templates with the new three categories ONLY - Utility, Authentication and Marketing. Any other category will not be accepted by Gupshup. They can create templates via -

the Gupshup Create Template interface

or the Gupshup Template Submit API for partners. Read updated API docs here.

or the WhatsApp Manager (for embedded signup users)

Managing migrated templates

Starting March 29, 2023 customers will start seeing the new along with the old category for their templates auto-migrated by Meta, in the below ways -

the Gupshup Template List interface

or the Gupshup Template list API for partners. Read updated API docs here

or the WhatsApp Manager (for embedded signup users)

Businesses will also receive 2 sub-types of Template event - Template status update event with the new category and Template category update event (if you have subscribed for template events). Read updated API docs here.

Managing rejected templates

Starting April 01, 2023, businesses can raise an appeal against the rejection or new auto-assigned category of templates by-

Writing to devsupport@gupshup.io (for traditionally onboarded WABAs)

or the WhatsApp Manager (for embedded signup users)

Managing billing and analytics

All conversations will continue to be charged as per User initiated and Business initiated conversations till May 31, 2023, no matter what category they are assigned. We will keep you notified about the changes and advancements on the wallet and analytics side in the coming weeks.

Gupshup fee changes

We understand you have enough to handle :) There will be no Gupshup fee changes in this timeline.
