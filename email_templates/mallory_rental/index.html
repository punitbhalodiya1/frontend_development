Reasons to work with Mallory Safety and Supply

To ensure that your equipment is working properly:

Worn or damaged safety equipment can fail to protect workers from hazards, which can lead to serious injuries or death. Mallory Safety and Supply's team of experts can inspect your equipment and make sure that it is in good working order. We will use our service records to alert you when your equipment needs calibration and regular maintenance.

To comply with safety regulations:

Safety regulations require employers to have their safety equipment repaired and calibrated on a regular basis. Mallory Safety and Supply can help you ensure that you are in compliance with all applicable regulations.

To save money:

Repairing or renting safety equipment from Mallory Safety and Supply can save you money in the long run. Buying new equipment can be expensive, repaired or rented equipment can be a more cost-effective option.

To get the peace of mind knowing that your workers are safe:

When you have your safety equipment repaired or rented from Mallory Safety and Supply, you can be confident that your workers are safe. We are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality service and ensuring that their workers are protected.
