Leading Edge: Don't Get Caught Off Guard

A Fall from a leading edge can be devastating...

Imagine this: a worker on a construction site, bustling with activity, loses their footing on a leading edge. The fall is sudden, the ground rushes closer. This scenario, unfortunately, becomes reality all too often. Leading edges, those ever-changing, unprotected edges on roofs, floors, and more, pose a significant threat to worker safety.

Don’t let a costly surprise detail your project

Falls from leading edges are not just devastating for workers; they can also derail your project with hefty fines, lawsuits, and delays. By prioritizing leading edge fall protection, you're not just protecting your workers, but also your business.
