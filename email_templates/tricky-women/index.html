We also support Tricky Women – International Animation Filmfestival.

Tricky Women - International Animation Filmfestival
7 – 11 March 2018

With film programmes, public talks, workshops, lectures, parties and exhibitions, TRICKY WOMEN is celebrating independent animation productions by women in Vienna’s METRO Kinokulturhaus. This year’s festival focuses on documentary and political animation films.

We’re giving away tickets for the opening gala on 7 March in Vienna’s Gartenbaukino, as well as for other selected film screenings.

We also support the Erste Bank ExtraVALUE Film Award.

In 2017, the Erste Bank ExtraVALUE Film Award, presented as part of the Viennale (Vienna International Film Festival), went to Ruth Kaaserer for her documentary Gwendolyn.

Jury statement

A wonderful, attentive film that captures the imagination from the very beginning. In a seemingly random manner, it unfolds a multilayered panorama of human relationships in different living environments. In a captivatingly clear dramatic structure, it tells the story of an outstanding, strong woman, whom we get to know step by step. The director succeeds in blurring the boundaries between documentary and feature film.

We’re giving away tickets for screenings of the film in Vienna’s Stadtkino im Künstlerhaus, starting on 16 March.

We also support Culture Transfair, a project by Hunger for Art and Culture.

Culture Transfair, a Hunger for Art and Culture project, promotes networking between cultural and social institutions and develops special mediation programmes between clients of social facilities.

As part of Culture Transfair 2017, the WIENER KUCHL (“Viennese Kitchen”) was developed in collaboration with MAK and “wiederwohnen” wohn:mobil.

In the course of the project, the prototype of a DIY kitchen and a construction manual were created. The current pilot phase, supervised by Fonds Soziales Wien / wohn:mobil, has been made possible thanks to Erste Bank’s ExtraVALUE Sponsoring Programme.

The “Wiener Kuchl” has been nominated for the SozialMarie prize for social innovation.

We also support the Caritas children’s campaign.

Caritas accompanies people in difficult life situations, regardless of their social status, nationality, or religious affiliation.
As part of a long-term collaboration with Caritas, Erste Bank and the savings banks have been supporting the two large donation campaigns, Home Relief and Caritas Children’s Campaign.
