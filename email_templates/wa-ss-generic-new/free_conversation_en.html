Hello user,

Its been a tough month for all, considering the recent WhatsApp pricing changes. We have tried our best to keep this transition as smooth as possible and strive hard to set up the most fair process in the same. With this idea, we’re soon coming with some major improvements related to ‘free tier’ conversations that you ‘rightfully deserve.

Here are some questions you may have and how we’re resolving them with respect to Free Tier Conversations -

Why are free tier conversations of February ’22 getting treated as paid?

WhatsApp does not provide a clear indication of free tier conversations synchronously, we only get access to this data at the end of the month. We have thus been deducting the amount at the beginning, and have planned to refund the same once we tally the amounts basis the end-of-month reports.

When will the deductions of free tier conversations in Feb ’22 be refunded and how?

The amount deducted for February’s free tier conversations will get refunded to your wallet as ‘Refund for Free tier’ in the first week of March

Going forward, will free tier conversations always be deducted at the beginning of the month?

Absolutely NO. This process will change w.e.f March 01, 2022

So how will free tier conversations be handled from March 01, 2022 ?

Effective March 01, 2022, the first 1000 conversations in your app will be considered as ‘Free Tier’ and the fee for it will not be deducted. Based on WhatsApp’s final reports received at the end of the month, the amount for these conversations will be adjusted (less or more) in your wallet. Note: Adjustment will be minimal but might be required mainly when one WABA has multiple phone numbers / Gupshup app linkages.

Will there be any changes in the billing event for March 01, 2022?

Yes, there will be impacting changes in the payload structure of billing-event. Read docs to start making your changes now.

Do reach out to devsupport@gupshup.io or your direct Customer Success Manager if there is an issue
