1) Embed registration is now available in Go live journey

Facebook’s embedded registration option is now available to all in the Go-live journey. You can choose the option if you don’t have a verified Facebook Business Manager and want to try the 30-day Unverified Trial Experience by Facebook. Know more

2) Header is now optional in Quick Replies

To bring in consistency with the WhatsApp implementation, we have now made the header optional in quick replies, which was previously mandatory. There is a change in curl for interactive session media. You can read about it here.

3) Audio files support in Customer Support tool

Our customer support tool now supports sending audio files to users.

File types supported: audio/aac, audio/mp4, audio/ogg, audio/mpeg, codecs=opus

(The base audio/ogg type is not supported by Facebook).

4) Cyrillic character support added

For our customers speaking languages that require Cyrillic character support in their script, are now supported on our platform.

5) Messaging tier API now available

Partners can now check messaging tier of their customers that is allowed by WhatsApp and act accordingly. Know more

Notice

Major WhatsApp outage on Oct 04, 2021

As you might be aware, on October 4, 2021, WhatsApp experienced a disruption to all their apps and products. Access to their services, including the WhatsApp Business API, Ads that Click to WhatsApp, and the WhatsApp Business App, have since been fully restored as confirmed by them. WhatsApp business messages (from both the WhatsApp Business API and WhatsApp Business App) did not deliver during the outage but were delivered since the outage was resolved. All messages that were scheduled to be sent during the outage have since been delivered, as per WhatsApp.

WhatsApp's recent email to partners said - "We know how challenging the inability to communicate with customers can be, and we are working to address what caused this system-wide outage to prevent this from happening again. We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience this caused and appreciate your understanding and patience during this time."

Once this outage was completed, Gupshup ensured that all enqueued messages and processes started flowing smoothly. If any of you faced issues related to this, please escalate to devsupport@gupshup.io.
