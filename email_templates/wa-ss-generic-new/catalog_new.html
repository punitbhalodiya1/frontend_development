We are happy to announce that WhatsApp’s catalog API is now available on our platform.

The Catalog API lets WhatsApp businesses showcase their products and/or services to their end users in the form of a catalogue and receive orders with the help of the 'Add to cart' feature. Here's how it looks like -

Steps to send catalog from your Gupshup app:
  1. Step 1 : Business creates a catalog on their Facebook Commerce Manager
  2. Step 2 : Business/ISV assigns Gupshup as partner business to their catalog
  3. Step 3 : Business (with support from devsupport team, link their WABA on Gupshup, to the catalog [One WABA - One Catalog only]
  4. Step 4 : Business then sends single or multiple product messages to customers

Note: If the WABA is Indian (+91 phone number), business’s e-commerce compliance is necessary. Please read - How to comply with the laws for selling online in India using WhatsApp Business API
