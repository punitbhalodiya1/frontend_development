We would like to introduce Select Buy and Build VI - Italy, (Group Video) an opportunity to invest in an Italy-based Buy and Build concept - Gruppo Florence - active in design, development, and production of high-end “Made in Italy” products for global luxury brands. The platform successfully started 1.5 years ago and advanced above expectations, having integrated 8 highly profitable companies across different verticals. The group already reached EUR 145m in consolidated revenues in 2021 with a high EBITDA margin of 20%. The strong pipeline of 15 companies intends to bolster vertical and horizontal expansion, bringing the company’s revenues to c. EUR 500m in 2022 with EBITDA in excess of EUR 100m.

Initially, Select secured an investment of EUR 4m in Gruppo Florence in an oversubscribed EUR 50m capital increase in June 2021. Due to the existing relationship with the partner, Select negotiated a larger allocation of EUR 10m in the new funding round to further support Florence’s Buy and Build strategy.

We are targeting a net IRR of >25% / 2x net MM and aim to return the capital after 2-3 years due to expected exit via IPO or trade sale as early as 2023.

Experienced team and sponsors: Led by Attila Kiss (Ermanno Scervino, Balenciaga) and Francesco Trapani (ex-CEO Bulgari; LVMH, Tiffany & Co., Tages), Gruppo Florence leverages their extensive operational experience as well as profound knowledge of the brand and consumer sectors. The team is supported by three leading Italian PE investors (VAM, FIIC, Italmobiliare), which invest their full pro-rata amounts in the current capital increase.

Sticky business model: The group develops luxury fashion products for top international fashion brands (e.g., Hermes, Chanel, Versace, and Burberry) and benefits from recurring revenues thanks to long-standing client relationships (often 5-20 years) as brands require the technological know-how of the local Italian companies.

Very attractive entry: Buy-in at 6.4x EV/EBITDA based on 2022 financials, well below the average European PE multiples and global brands multiples. The business is very cash generative which allows for a double-digit FCF yield.

Multiple arbitrage: Through the creation of a larger platform, Gruppo Florence benefits from an immediate multiple arbitrage as comparable larger platforms are typically valued at 9-11x EV/EBITDA.

Robust pipeline: Florence has a pipeline of 15 targets to be acquired at c. 6x EV/EBITDA in place to lift the revenue to EUR 500m.

Strong organic growth: The global luxury market grows at 6% p.a. (double of global GDP), profiting from the increasing consumption of luxury goods by the expanding emerging market middle classes.

The total deal size is EUR 10m and we are looking to firm up investor commitments in the next 2-3 weeks with the first capital call of 60% expected to be in early April and the second capital end of the year.
