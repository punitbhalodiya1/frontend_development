Here's a rundown of the recent events at Gupshup!

Features

New sign up process

Users must now specify whether they were introduced to Gupshup by the Gupshup Sales team, an ISV / Gupshup partner, or directly upon signup, and then input their company data.

Template sync button added

Template list page now includes a button for syncing Gupshup templates with Facebook business manager

Provision to set multiple callback urls

Businesses may now choose from a variety of callback URLs depending on their needs. You can read about it here.

New template categories are updated on the template submission page

This is to inform you that WhatApp has recently provided a change in template submission process, now keeping only 3 supported template categories to select from, namely -

MARKETING – To send promotional offers, product announcements, and more to increase awareness and engagement

ONE-TIME PASSWORDS (OTP) – To send promotional offers, product announcements, and more to increase awareness and engagement

TRANSACTIONAL – To send promotional offers, product announcements, and more to increase awareness and engagement

Improvements

Dashboard and settings page loading times have been improved.
The time needed to load these pages has improved by 23% & 47% respectively.

Bug Fixes

Marking inbound messages as read

There were problems with marking new messages as read, but those have now been fixed.
