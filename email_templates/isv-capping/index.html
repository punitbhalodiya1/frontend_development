New Capping Limit

That is correct! We have reduced our monthly capping limit* of 750 USD to 75 USD* per app for ISV’s linked apps (*maximum amount you pay as Gupshup fees, WhatsApp fee at actuals)

Things to note:
1.

There is no separate manual process to get the new capping limit, except that the apps must be linked to your partner ID.
2.

For March ‘22, capping will apply from the 1st of March and the extra Gupshup fee deductions will be manually adjusted by 10th April ‘22 along with self serve UI changes.
3.

From April '22 onwards, the capping will apply from the same date, when the apps are linked to partner ID. There will be no future adjustments done if app linking is delayed.
4.

This refund process will be automated w.e.f April ‘22.
5.

There will be daily refund for Gupshup fee if monthly billing has crossed the capping and there have been deductions.
6.

Per message fee by Gupshup remains the same as 0.001 USD
