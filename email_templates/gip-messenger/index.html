What is GIP messenger?

We have all heard about the various possibilities of GIP (Gupshup IP messaging) in several forums by now. And GIP messenger app takes it one step further. GIP Messenger is an A2P app that connects businesses and users, where users can chat with their favorite brand bots, get latest product news, buy products, view discount offers, receive business notifications and much more.

What device can I test this on?

Currently the app is only supported on Android version 8.1 and above. So if you own an iOS or other device, do ask out your friends for an Android phone if you want to win some cool prizes. :)

How can I set up the app?
1

From your Android phone, click on this link to download the app. While downloading the app you might get 2 options to open the file: Google Drive or your default browser, Select Google Drive.
2

On downloading the app you may get an option to Install the app. If your phone asks your permission to allow app installation from unknown sources then tap “Settings”> enable the switch next to “Allow from this source” on the following screen. Then go back and resume installation.
3

Once your app is ready to use, go ahead and try it in all the ways possible.
