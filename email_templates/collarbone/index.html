Castles and Concert's
Annual Dinner and Show

You are invited to join your friends from past or future adventures for an evening of fine food. great company and the best music that you'ill find. Billy and Amy valentine join us from LA and Billy tells us That he has a brand new Song that he Will perform for the first time just for us. Matt and chip are busy adding new entertainers to our roster of travelling guest performers. Among them are Barnaby Finch who has toured with George Benson and lee Ritenour for more than two decades and Vince Littleton who can he heard on CDs with jerry Garcia, Bonnie Raitt, john Lee Hooker and many others. Also new to the group is Grammy award winner, Aaron Lington. He heads the Jazz program at San jose State and his performing and compositional credits include collaborations with the San Francisco Symphony, Maynard Ferguson. The BBC Radio Orchestra. the Count Basic Orchestra, Doc Severinsen and many more. All of these folks will be there, plus a grand group of entertainers that you already know well, includling Terry Miller, David Jackson, Matt Eakle, jeff Sanford and several others. It will quite a show and we hope that you'll join us. If you've been telling your friends about Castles and Concerts, you are welcome to bring them along and introduce them to the group.

University Club, Mason and California (SF)

Cocktails at 5:30, Show at 6:30, Dinner at 7:30
$45/person include wine with dinner. Cocktails are no-host

Budapest, Bavaria and Beyond-
Castles & Concerts on The Blue Danube

We are just eight (8) weeks away from our first dinner in enchanting Budapest on October 21, and we cannot wait to be with you on our Fall 2018 Trip. After a few days of activities and parties, including a special concert with the "house" orchestra of one of the last privately-held palaces in the old city. we board the luxury riverboat Avalon Panorama, for our journey through old-world Vienna and the charming cities of Durnstein and Melk, in the picturesque Wachau valley.
