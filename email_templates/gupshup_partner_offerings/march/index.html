Meta Updates

Frequency Capping for India

META launched a new integrity check on 06 Feb 2024, called 'Frequency Capping'. This check ensures that an Indian end user (with a +91 phone number) can only receive messages from 'X' brands in a rolling Y-day window. (META is dynamically deciding the x and y and is not to be known to businesses or BSPs). This feature might roll out globally once the tests are completed.

Read rate consideration from April 01

META wants to empower businesses to send more engaging and relevant marketing messages. Thus they will start considering read rates alongside traditional metrics like blocks and reports. Campaigns with low read rates may be temporarily paused, starting April 1, 2024, allowing businesses to refine templates before scaling volume. This will provide businesses time to iterate on the templates with the lowest engagement before scaling volume.
