QBM - Quality Based Messages

The idea of a "quality-based message" was introduced internationally by WhatsApp as part of their global launch initiative to make WhatsApp the most preferred communication channel. This feature enables companies to participate in full-funnel dialogues with their clients. This meant that the limitation of communications outside of the 24-hour timeframe being 'transactional-only' was abolished.

Even while they may have a promotional tone, the messages may not really be commercial at all; rather, they may be what are known as "quasi-promotional" communications or non-transactional messages such as:

Product recommendations

Back-in-stock notifications

Time based offerings

Important updates
