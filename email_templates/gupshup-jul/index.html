WABA migration live now!

Migration allows WhatsApp phone numbers to be migrated from one BSP to another BSP without creating any downtime in sending messages. The phone number keeps its display name, quality status, messaging limits, Official Business Account status, and any high quality message templates previously approved. All you need to do is get your existing BSP to disable the two factor authentication on the WABA and you’re ready to migrate!
