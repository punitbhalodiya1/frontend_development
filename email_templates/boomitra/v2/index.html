Spotlight Story

Jorge Pando, a young Mexican rancher, breathed new life into his struggling ranch using regenerative land management practices. With the expert support of Boomitra and SolucionesGanaderas (Ranching Solutions), Jorge transformed barren soil into a thriving ecosystem, rich with biodiversity and improved soil moisture.

“We began with a 2200-hectare pasture, then we broke it into 4 paddocks, then the evolution began,” Jorge told us. “Now we have 124 paddocks of about 16 hectares each, divided by electrical solar-powered fences.” He mentioned, “the 124 paddocks are further divided into smaller sections, for a total of 2976 paddocks.' Each paddock gets over 18 months of rest before it is grazed again.

Jorge told us, “It is very impressive how refreshed the soil is compared to the old management after only two years.”

“For my father, many other people, and me, these words mean future. A project like this is very important and very difficult at the same time because it requires a big change, yet it is the only path forward.”
