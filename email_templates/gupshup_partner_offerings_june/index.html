Template automatic categorization - Webhook and UI

Starting June 1, 2024, to ensure that all templates are correctly categorized, Meta has implemented a recurring process that automatically identifies and updates the category of any marketing or utility templates that have been miscategorized, according to their guidelines. Know more

Starting July 15, Gupshup will be sending this event to your callback if it is subscribed for template events. Read doc here

Get Quality Rating Update

We've updated the response for the Get Quality Rating API to now include a new field called Phone Quality, with the following possible values:

Green: High quality

Yellow: Medium quality

Red: Low quality

Click here to know more

Default Gupshup Automated Optin Message Disabled for New Apps

As informed in our communication ‘Important Updates : Gupshup Partner Offerings’, we have changed the default behaviour of the Gupshup triggered automated optin message. It will be ‘OFF’ by default for all new apps created. Behaviour set for already created apps will not change. Know more

Launch of Authentication Conversations in India and Authentication International Rate Card in India and Indonesia

Meta has updated their authentication rate card for Indonesia in June 2024 and made authentication available in India in July 2024. Know more

They also introduced a new authentication-international rate for these countries in the same time. Know more

These rate cards are now supported on Gupshup. Note that existing authentication rates across all markets are not changing.

Wallet Statements in IST timezone:

As informed in our communication ‘Important Updates : Gupshup Partner Offerings’, wallet statements are now available in IST time zone only, for the time being. Past and recent wallet statements downloaded will also be in IST time zone. Usage section will continue to work in selected time zone. Know more

Kazakhstan pricing correction

Effective immediately, pricing for services in Kazakhstan are now adhering to the rates outlined in Meta's official pricing rate card, ensuring standardized billing for our partners in the region.
