Here are a few recent developments -

Monthly capping automation

A few months ago we had introduced a default upper limit (capping) of 750 USD worth Gupshup billing for every app. We’re now automating this process, in case you cross the limit. The refund will be recredited to the same wallet every day at 12am Brazil time, instead of waiting till the end of the month. The process is effective from Sep 01, 2021. ( Note : The apps will need to maintain balance for a day )

Direct 10 USD Overdraft

Users with at least 1 live WhatsApp app, now get a direct overdraft of 10 USD in their Gupshup wallet. This means even if your wallet balance goes in negative, your usage can continue to up to minus 10 USD. You can refill this overdraft when you recharge next time

Hosting option in Germany

Businesses can now choose to store their data in Germany or India while making their app live. Read about our GDPR compliance here
