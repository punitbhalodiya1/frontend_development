About

For the film industry, we offer modern production services, innovative workflow solutions and the transfer of know-how in times of strong technological changes and new business models.

transfermedia production services is an one-stop-agency for intertwining storytelling and tech. Based at the renowned Babelsberg studios in Germany, transfermedia production services has an international network of media professionals, creatives, technologists, institutions and universities.

Based on more than 20 years of experience in production we are providing full scale production service – from script to master, for feature films, documentaries, commercials and image films. Whether for cinema, TV, VoD platforms or web.

In a changing digital ecosystem for all media transfermedia production services is as well developing new technologies like the FILM DATA BOX. We are initiator and co-organizer of the international technology conference MTH Conference which takes place annually in November.

Together with the London-based Digital Production Partnership network, we are the publishers of the first international FILM TECH APP for media professionals, which we completely revised in 2019. We initiate and lead media technology R&D-projects. We share our knowledge together with colleagues in seminars and workshops.
