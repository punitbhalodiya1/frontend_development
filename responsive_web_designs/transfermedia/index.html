Media Technologies

Scanning, copying, checking and providing transcoded footage in the cloud for viewing and editing. All of this in a short time and fully automated: that is the FILM DATA BOX. With the FILM DATA BOX you work securely, collaboratively and bring the real value of your work into a transparent, structured organisation. In this way, you also fulfil all the requirements of clients and insurers for your data management. The FILM DATA BOX was developed by media professionals and is available for sale at MBF Filmtechnik.
