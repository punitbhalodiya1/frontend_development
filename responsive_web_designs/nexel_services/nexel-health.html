Nexel Health

The Gateway to Better Healthcare

explore more

Providing quality healthcare has never been more critical. An aging population coupled with a far longer life expectancy than ever before places unexpected strain on a clearly unprepared healthcare system.

If nothing else, the pandemic proved that the healthcare system needs more and better support to serve the end-user more effectively.

This is where Nexel Health comes in. A wholly-owned subsidiary of Nexel Services, we bring the best of the best to the healthcare industry.

We share the same vision as our parent company - to revolutionize the healthcare industry by embedding our culture of excellence and growth and doing it OUR way.

We are not beholden to the “traditional” way of doing things, nor are we tied down by a limiting mindset. At heart, we are innovators with a passion for making the world a better place.

That is why the Nexel Health team is wholly committed to providing the best possible products and services to ensure that the healthcare industry can deliver the outstanding quality care patients deserve, regardless of the circumstances.

We work through our three divisions to assist healthcare providers and patients by improving effectiveness and delivering the best-in-class care.

Through our medical billing services, Nexel Health focuses on supporting medical professionals and their practices. We enable healthcare providers to focus on what really matters, namely providing outstanding care, while we handle the billing, coding, revenue cycle management, and credentialing.

Nexel Health also provides top-quality, durable medical equipment, thereby supporting medical professionals in their quest to ensure a better quality of life for their patients. With a wide range of excellent products, we ensure that your patients get all the support they need to enjoy the best possible quality of life.

At Nexel Health, we believe that the key to success lies in working together to revolutionize the healthcare industry and provide the best quality care that patients deserve. Only together can we take healthcare to the NEXt levEL!
