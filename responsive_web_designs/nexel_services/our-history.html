Our History

OUR HISTORY

the vision and innovative talents

An ambitious man who suffered many failures had a vision to create a company where young, innovative people with vision could apply their talents in the service of others

In late September 2016, Nexel was formed, and by January 2017, the founder of this new start-up had drawn together a group of young individuals who had a knack for getting things done, but also the vision and innovative talents necessary to thrive in today’s business world.
